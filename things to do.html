Things to do in Žilina

Žilina offers a variety of attractions and activities. Popular options include visiting Budatín Castle and exploring the historic Marianske Square. For those interested in culture and history, the Holy Trinity Cathedral and the Považské Múzeum (a regional museum) are worth visiting. Nature lovers can enjoy the surrounding region with its castles and hiking opportunities.

Here's a more detailed look at some specific things to do:

  • Budatín Castle: A well-preserved castle with a museum and beautiful grounds.
  • Marianske Square: The main square in Zilina, offering a glimpse into the city's architecture and history.
  • Holy Trinity Cathedral: A notable church in the city center.
  • Považské Múzeum: The regional museum, showcasing the history and culture of the area.
  • Burian Tower: A historical Renaissance tower offering views of the city.
  • Lietava Castle: Ruins of a medieval castle with scenic views.
  • Stanica Žilina-Záriečie: A cultural center and former train station, now hosting events and exhibitions.
